Volunteer Scheduling Application

A web-based scheduler that lets people sign themselves into role-scoped, capacity-limited activities on a shared weekly calendar — with private/public jobs & events, per-activity limits, and role-based visibility for schedulers, supervisors, and administrators.

  • Self-service scheduling with per-activity limits
  • Administrator, Scheduler, and Viewer/Supervisor roles
  • Branding & terminology configurable per organization
  • Layered, server-enforced security

About
